DINE @THE ZOO

If you're craving a unique dining experience in Durban, Dine @ the Zoo is a perfect blend of wildlife charm and cozy cafés. At Mitchell Park in Morningside, the Blue Zoo Café & Tea Garden offers a peaceful garden setting near a mini zoo, complete with birdsong, weekend piano melodies, and the occasional mischievous monkey. Their menu delights with classics like lamb curry, scones, and waffles—perfect for a relaxed family outing. Further out in Cato Ridge, the Zoo Café at Natal Zoological Gardens brings even more adventure with wood-fired pizzas, pancakes, and burgers, all served inside the zoo grounds.
Open daily from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, this spot also features braai facilities, picnic areas, and a splash pad for kids, making it ideal for a full day of animal encounters and fresh air. Whether you're sipping tea among peacocks or grabbing a slice after a monkey sighting, dining at the zoo is an experience that blends the wild with the wonderful.
