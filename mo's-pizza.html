MO'S PIZZA

Moe’s Pizza in Durban is your go-to spot for authentic New York–style pizza, sizzling steaks, creamy milkshakes, and decadent desserts. Located at 50 Cowey Road, this cozy eatery blends bold flavors with a warm, welcoming vibe. Here’s what you might love:

- Opening Hours:
- Mon: 12:30 PM – 7:30 PM
- Tues–Thurs: 12:00 PM – 8:00 PM
- Fri: 2:00 PM – 9:00 PM
- Sat: 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM
- Sun: 12:30 PM – 8:00 PM
- Vibe: A stylish, fire-powered kitchen with a passion for pizza science and quality ingredients—think stone-ground flour and imported tomatoes.
- Extras: You can dine in, order online, or even host a pizza party there.
